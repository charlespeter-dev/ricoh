Leverage Hybrid Cloud Technology to Maximise Business Value

As companies increasingly rely on technology to power their businesses, the need for a robust and flexible IT infrastructure has become more critical than ever. With the rise of cloud computing, organisations are increasingly adopting a hybrid cloud approach that combines the best of both worlds—the flexibility and scalability of public cloud services and the control and security of private cloud solutions.

A hybrid cloud enables companies to seamlessly move workloads and data between different cloud environments and choose the right cloud for each workload based on cost, security, and performance. However, managing a hybrid cloud can be daunting, and companies must ensure they have the expertise and resources to take full advantage of this technology.

Almost two-thirds of spending on application software will be directed toward cloud technologies in 2025.”
Gartner, 2022

With Ricoh, you can unleash your business’s full potential and stay ahead of the digital curve.

Robust cloud solutions for enhanced hosting and data centres

As the digitalisation of data and devices continues to disrupt organisations, many struggle to keep up with the infrastructure agility and flexibility required to effectively consolidate, automate, standardise, and virtualise their systems.

With comprehensive cloud solutions for hosting and data centres, you can revitalise your business and gain a competitive edge. Whether you need to enhance your hosting capabilities, streamline your data centre operations, or leverage the power of the cloud for your business needs, our complete cloud solutions have you covered.

Seamless migration services with minimal disruption

Cloud infrastructure can bring significant benefits to your business, but not all applications or data are suitable for migration. There are key factors that you need to take into consideration such as business continuity and resource availability, among other things.

At Ricoh, we understand the challenges and risks involved in migration, which is why we offer seamless migration services. Our team of experts will work with you to assess your needs and develop a customised migration plan that ensures minimal downtime, maximum security, and optimised performance. With our help, you can confidently transition to the cloud and start reaping the benefits of modern infrastructure.

IBM, 2022
