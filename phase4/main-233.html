Seamless Connectivity at the Speed of Light

Connectivity is the driving force that combines data, information, services, and products, into one seamless experience for businesses and their customers.

A well designed network provides mobility for work and processes, capacity to deliver various loads efficiently, and accessibility to all who require it, with little to no downtime.

Our expertise in network design, deployment, monitoring, and optimisation will help your network run smoothly for employees and customers, ensuring a seamless experience and uninterrupted business continuity.

Rev Up Your Network With High-Performance Services

Your network serves as the foundation for connecting your business to the world. As interconnectivity needs increase and devices become more sophisticated, the risk of bottlenecks and slowdowns grows. Additionally, dependable network connectivity is crucial to managing complex internal and external relationships across multiple platforms and channels.

You need the right tools, skills, and knowledge to ensure performance, stability, and security and to optimise costs. That’s where Ricoh comes in. We offer:

Advanced real-time monitoring and management tools

Comprehensive ticketing tools and SLA measurements

Access to 24/7 service support

Support from 1st to 3rd level technicians and engineering specialists

Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) methodologies.

With our tailored network connectivity solutions, we can analyse your specific needs and help you build a custom networking infrastructure that solves your business challenges and keeps your customers satisfied. From devices to security and networks to operations, we bring everything together to create a smarter, future-ready IT ecosystem where everything flows seamlessly—so you can stay connected at all times.

Our Network & Internet Solutions

Ricoh’s Network & Internet solutions comprise two key offerings: Network Services and Internet, Data & Voice.

Network Services

Your organisation is a complex mix of people, information, and systems. When there’s a disruption in connectivity, it can cause major problems for workflows, teamwork, and customer service.

We offer you a comprehensive suite of network services from leading network technology providers. With our extensive experience and expertise, we can provide you with the network connectivity and flexibility you need to thrive both now and in the future.

Internet, Data & Voice

Ensure smooth and stable connections with the right internet, data and voice solutions. Stay ahead of the curve by supporting high levels of connectivity, collaboration, and cutting-edge applications like cloud computing and video conferencing that prioritise both uploading and downloading.
