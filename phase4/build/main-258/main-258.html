Our Approach

Efficiency and connection

We’re committed to improving and simplifying your work life by empowering the digital workplace. How do we do it?

Our method starts with understanding the unique needs of your business and providing you with select choices. Once these new systems and processes are running, we continuously track and recalibrate them for ongoing peak performance.

Making it happen

Our IT specialists understand that change is bigger than technology. It’s about the people, processes, and discovering new ways to solve old problems.

The business of change improves work life through smarter workplace technology that creates positive developments. You can embark on the journey to progress in four practical steps, adapted to suit your particular needs.

Our four-step approach will get you on the digital transformation journey from where you are now. We’ll guide you towards solutions that span communications, mobility, digitisation, and automation—tailored to suit your people and customers.

We understand your business

Selling solutions is not enough. With Ricoh by your side, reap the benefits of consultants and solution specialists that become part of your business family. Our focus lies in having a comprehensive view of your team’s goals. We’ll drive the outcomes that support what matters most to your organisation.

Four Steps to Exceptional IT Effectiveness

Map your business needs

We gather your varying business goals to identify the technology you need for today and tomorrow. This helps us understand the core challenges you need to address in delivering operational efficiency and seamless customer experience.

Co-creation and design-a-service solution

When the capabilities you need are identified, our specialists plan a mix of digital workforce and workspace platforms that can help spur productivity and cultivate human engagement. This enables you to meet communication, automation and collaboration goals while removing barriers to knowledge and data sharing.

Identify quick wins and priorities

We will connect with your people and customers to pinpoint workflow gaps and build a project plan, including quick wins to boost engagement in the business of positive change. As 83% of people believe that the right technology improves work life for employees, you can be assured that what’s good for your people is great for your organisation.

Engage and educate with an iterative pathway

Our professionals will set a plan of action in motion, combining the right mix of new technologies, workplace practices, and attitudes on how work gets done. By breaking down a robust digital transformation journey into manageable steps, we can help you realise positive change.
