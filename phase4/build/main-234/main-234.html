Boost Your Cybersecurity Posture in a Dynamic Digital World

IT teams today are dealing with two of the biggest cybersecurity challenges:

  • Providing employees with seamless connection and peak network performance
  • Keeping up with the escalating threats in an increasingly digital world by maintaining strong security protocols.

They need to securely enable the growing universe of roaming users, devices, and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) applications. This must be achievable without adding complexities or dropping end-user performances while leveraging current security investments.

On the other hand, a uniform level of network performance and security protection for users is necessary wherever they are. It is vital to have a comprehensive strategy for your systems and platform to withstand various threats. This way, end-users can work securely, regardless of location or device.

Ricoh can help you maintain business continuity and improve the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data.

Protect Your Business. Protect Your Customers.

Our Network Security Solutions

Secure Access Service Edge (SASE) and Zero Trust Networking Access (ZTNA)

The future of network security is SASE and ZTNA. This architecture converges networking and security functions in the cloud, so you can connect your users to the applications and data they need, from wherever it resides to wherever they are.

SASE, a building block of ZTNA, allows you to:

  • Securely connect your workforce only to the applications they need and nothing more, eliminating lateral threat movement by a compromised user
  • Control access through simplified security and policy enforcement
  • Meet multi-cloud demands at scale by converging networking and security functions.
