Elevate Hybrid Collaboration and Productivity

In a world moving towards hybrid working as the norm, it is more important than ever for businesses to find new and innovative ways to collaborate. Teams are expected to be highly productive but, at the same time, be flexible and agile enough to respond quickly to changes in the workplace.

While organisations increasingly use unified communication platforms to connect and interact with people, many teams still find it frustrating to communicate effectively using different collaboration tools. To stay ahead of the competition, you need a meeting solution that can help you keep up with the fast-paced, ever-changing work environment.

Deploy an All-in-One Collaborative Meeting Solution

A versatile, all-in-one meeting solution, such as Ricoh’s Creative Collaboration Hub, is designed to foster collaboration, increase productivity, and deliver a consistent user experience. The hub unifies communications and provides the tools necessary for teams to communicate and work together effectively. This makes it the ideal solution for organisations who want to improve their communication and collaboration both in-office and remotely.

Enrich Collaboration with Ricoh Creative Collaboration Hub

The Ricoh Creative Collaboration Hub is a powerful, interactive meeting solution that enables meaningful collaboration, regardless of the participant’s location, device, or unified communication platforms.

Experience more productive meetings with this all-in-one meeting solution that includes video conferencing support for up to ten platforms–ending the hassle of last-minute meeting app downloads and connection issues. This robust hub encourages creativity and productivity by enabling all employees to also contribute via mobile devices.

The central collaborative hub:

  • Delivers a welcome screen and modern, user-friendly interface
  • Offers touchless wake-up with motion detection
  • Supports all Microsoft Windows 10 applications
  • Provides easy content-sharing with laptop or smartphone via wired, wireless, and QR code
  • Presents powerful canvas–browser, video, and image on live-screen
  • Turns your notes into digital content via remote whiteboarding
  • Enables a single touch-to-join on any Unified Communications platform
  • Supports MS Teams, Zoom, Cisco WebEx, Google Meet, and others
  • Includes an AI-driven, voice-enabled Virtual Assistant
