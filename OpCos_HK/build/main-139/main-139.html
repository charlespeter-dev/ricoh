Transforming your workplace digitally

Building resilient and adaptable workplaces and workflows is something that many companies struggle to get right. There are so many platforms to integrate, so many use cases to navigate, and endless requirements driven by the increasing demands for hybrid solutions.

Our Workplace Services provides a holistic approach to design, planning, consulting and implementation of a digital workplace that will help drive your employees to work happily and securely. We can integrate your current tech stack with best-in-class partner solutions-all brought together in an interface that makes everything easy to monitor and manage.

Experience how Ricoh is empowering organisations to improve and transform your work life, share content with powerful collaboration tools and access information from virtually anywhere, in any form.

Workplace Design Services

Your business is complex, but your digital workplace shouldn’t be.

Our Digital Workplace Services inspires work style innovation by creating a workplace that enhances collaboration, creativity, and productivity through the optimisation of people, space, and technologies. Throughout our engagement, we provide full advisory, design, implementation and change management assistance for office space optimisation and streamlined work processes.

Managed Workplace Services

Our Managed Workplace Services enables workplace collaboration with IoT, analytics and support services, so you can deliver a seamless employee experience now and in the future.

By bringing workplace management under one roof, our team of experts can help your employees to work hassle-free. Whether you need a hand for day-to-day maintenance, support services to optimise your office resources, or preventive checking to keep your work efficient, we are here to empower your team to succeed in every step of the digital journey.

Digital Facilities Management

Enhancing workplace experience by digitising your facility management (FM) functions.

Our Digital Facilities Management Services is your one-stop solution to address workspace capacity challenges, support workplace safety, and drive energy savings without compromising employees’ experience.

It provides the full spectrum of FM tasks including:

  • Space management
  • Preventive maintenance
  • Visitor access management
  • On-site printing
  • IT, AV, and IoT management.
Discover now

Smart Lighting Management

Our Smart Lighting Management is a wireless lighting control system designed to enable intelligent office lighting control for optimised energy reduction and operational efficiency. Since no wiring is required, installation can be done 70% faster than wired solutions. Users can adjust the system with any smart device to improve work comfort.

Discover now

Video Surveillance and Analytics

Turning data into actionable insights for better growth.

Our Video Surveillance & Analytics (VSS) solution is a cloud-based video analytics solution that optimises business operations with real-time reporting and event-handling. Primarily used in retail, logistics, and commercial buildings, it is widely adopted to prevent thefts, mitigate crimes and improve facility operations.
