DHL Case Study

Using Logistics Document Automation to Simplify Processing

Manual data entry is draining productivity from logistics operations across APAC. When delivery notes, customs paperwork, and proof of delivery forms require manual processing, errors multiply, processing slows down, and your teams spend hours triple-checking instead of delivering.

So how are forward-thinking logistics operators maintaining accuracy and speed while controlling costs?

They're automating document processing with cloud-based solutions that integrate seamlessly with their existing systems.

DHL Supply Chain Iberia reduced manual work by 75% and virtually eliminated errors through cloud-based document management—transforming their delivery note processing almost overnight. What previously required two full-time employees to manage the whole day, now takes just one person working half a day.
