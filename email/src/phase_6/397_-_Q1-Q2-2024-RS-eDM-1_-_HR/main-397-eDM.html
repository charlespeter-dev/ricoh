If you find managing hybrid work while maintaining productivity and retaining staff challenging, you’re not alone. According to EY’s 2023 Future Workplace Index, 32% of companies identify optimising workspaces as their greatest challenge.
Issues like inefficient desk bookings, cumbersome room management, and a lack of real-time workplace insights can lead to decreased productivity, frustrated employees, and high turnover rates. So, how do you overcome these challenges to create a ‘workplace that works’?

Learn how a global pharmaceutical company overcame these challenges using RICOH Spaces to create an adaptable modern, hybrid-focused workplace that enhanced employee experience with:
Easy booking for desks, car parks, and meeting rooms that help reduce frustration and save time
Touchless sign-in for visitors with real-time host notifications to ensure smooth guest experiences and a secure visitor record
Real-time insights into space utilisation and working practices to improve workspace efficiency and employee satisfaction.
