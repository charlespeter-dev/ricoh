RDx Empowers Employee Creativity and Collaboration With RICOH Spaces

According to Gallup, 80% of remote-capable employees expect to work hybrid or remotely. This shift has made maintaining IT infrastructure to support onsite, hybrid, and remote teams an ongoing and complex challenge.
Balancing network security, user access, and space usage while ensuring seamless collaboration across locations is crucial. Neglecting these issues can decrease productivity and create data privacy concerns. So, how you can you address these challenges and maintain workspace efficiency and security?

Find out how Ricoh Digital Experience (RDx) overcame these challenges with RICOH Spaces, building a secure system whilst creating an efficient work environment with:
A scalable platform with IoT and advanced sensors that create a seamless, collaborative employee experience.
Contactless sign-in which ensures secure, enhanced visitor experiences while retaining a secure visitor record.
Comprehensive data and analytics that help optimise space and network usage.
