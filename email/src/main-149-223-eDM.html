In an increasingly competitive and fast-paced business landscape, technologies such as Robotic Process Automation (RPA) have become a key enabler of efficient and resilient operations. It empowers businesses to simplify complex workloads faster and easier than ever before. But how does RPA work?
Using software robots, or ‘bots,’ RPA automates traditionally repetitive, labour-intensive tasks and time-consuming manual processes for optimised operations. These bots can be programmed to perform various work, from data entry to processing customer service requests. According to AI Multiple, 70% to 80% of rules-based processes can be automated using RPA tools. This helps improve accuracy and save costs while freeing your employees to focus on higher value work.
