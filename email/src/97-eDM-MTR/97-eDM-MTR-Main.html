As more organisations pivot towards a hybrid approach to work, they need solutions that enable seamless collaboration between employees, no matter where they are. But with many rooms systems designed primarily for people in the room, it can leave remote users feeling left out.

Ricoh’s Microsoft Teams Rooms offer the perfect solution—a simple, effective way to deliver inclusive meeting experiences with all participants in mind. With a rich ecosystem of connected meeting devices powered by an intuitive app built for Microsoft Teams, Teams Rooms turn any meeting room into an easy-to-use and inclusive collaboration space where everyone has a seat at the table, regardless of where they are.
