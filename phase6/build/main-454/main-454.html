Maximise the Efficiency of Your Hybrid Workplace

Empower your team for true flexibility and extend the functionality of your RICOH multifunction printers (MFP) with RICOH Smart Integration. Streamline workflows, secure documents, and enhance productivity with direct integration to leading cloud services such as SharePoint, Google Drive, OneDrive for Business, Dropbox, and more.

Anytime, Anywhere, Any Device Flexibility

RICOH Smart Integration (RSI) digitises your document workflows for productivity, security, and efficiency. Your team can send a print request on the go and start the job only when they’re physically at the RICOH MFP. Key documents can be converted into editable and searchable formats when scanning to reduce wait and search times, while robust authentication protocols secure access and printing privileges.

RSI empowers your team by:

  • Automating repetitive manual workflows so your team can focus on more strategic tasks
  • Safeguarding documents against unauthorised access through secure access protocols and enabling print release from any device within the office
  • Simplifying print and scan processes for increased efficiency and productivity.

How RSI Works

RSI offers a modular approach that enables you to choose the functionalities best suited to your needs. It comprises RSI Connectors, RSI Control, and RSI Control+.

RSI Connectors

Inefficient document workflows can hinder your growth. RSI Connectors automate document workflows and integrate your MFPs with popular cloud services, saving you precious time and resources. Featuring the broadest integration with various cloud services, including OneDrive for Business, SharePoint, Google Drive, Box, Dropbox, and more, it simplifies and augments your workflows for efficiency and productivity.

Key benefits

  • Increase efficiency by automating time-consuming
    manual workflows
  • Digitise documents and convert them into editable and
    searchable files
  • Scan and save to platforms or select folders easily.

RSI Control and Control+

Managing your print infrastructure can be complex and time-consuming. Take charge of your print infrastructure with RSI Control and RSI Control+. Simplify, monitor, and streamline your cloud print management easily through a single platform that eases print management oversight while strengthening security.

Key benefits

  • Remove print servers and desktop client requirements,
    decreasing IT burdens
  • Increase accountability with strict authentication protocols
  • Simplify device management through a unified interface for
    efficient administration
  • Get visibility on print usage through reporting to effectively
    control costs.
