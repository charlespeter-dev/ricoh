Ricoh Interactive Whiteboard Add-On Service for Microsoft Office 365

The rise of hybrid work makes efficient and seamless collaboration tools a critical requirement for modern workplaces. One vital aspect of this is ensuring that meeting rooms are optimally set up to facilitate successful meetings. With Ricoh’s Interactive Whiteboards (IWB) Add-On Service for Office 365, you can enhance your meeting room setup to meet the demands of today’s collaborative work environments.

The Ricoh IWB Add-On Service for Office 365 is designed to enhance your existing setup with cloud-based features that integrate seamlessly with Microsoft Office 365. By simply tapping your card on the interactive whiteboard, you can access the files you need to communicate and collaborate with your colleagues.

Optimise Your Meeting Room Setup with our 10-Point Checklist

Meeting rooms are vital in a hybrid work environment. To unlock their full potential as hubs for collaboration, innovation, and decision-making, you need to optimise the efficiency of use and take an employee-focused approach to functionality.

Here is a checklist to determine if your current meeting room setup hits the mark for seamless collaboration:

Optimise Your Meeting Room Checklist

  1. Can attendees easily log in to the meeting system using their identification?

  2. Is it possible to retrieve and present meeting contents without connecting your laptop?

  3. Can files be accessed/loaded/saved instantly to your personal OneDrive folders?

  4. Are meeting annotations easily shareable to all attendees via email or OneDrive?

  5. Is setup quick and easy, with meeting notes instantly circulated to all attendees?

  6. Is there integration with Teams and SharePoint for enhanced communication?

  7. Are clear instructions provided for logging in and accessing materials?

  8. Is the room equipped to support these features?

  9. Is internet connectivity reliable for online platforms?

  10. Are there potential barriers to seamless setup and collaboration?

If you answered ‘no’ to any of the questions above, then you should consider ‘upskilling’ your meeting rooms. The good news is that the solution may be right in front of you.

Enhance Your Meeting Room Setup With Cloud-Based Features

From the time you tap onto the Ricoh IWB, our Add-On Service for Office 365 syncs and transfers you to a meeting experience where files are easily accessed, annotations are shared instantly, and more, without your laptop.

Easy start to your meetings

Start your meeting effortlessly by swiping your badge or card to log into Microsoft Office 365 and activate the IWB.

No laptop required

Retrieve and present your meeting’s contents from your Outlook scheduler or OneDrive right on the IWB. Virtual participants can see the IWB with the “Screen Share” function.

Always-on access to files

Access, load, and save the files and documents used instantly to your personal OneDrive folder during the meeting, ensuring everyone can see the latest changes made on the IWB.

Effortless sharing of annotations

Once the meeting has ended, share annotations and the updated files easily with all your attendees via email or OneDrive.

Fast and painless setup

Set up your meeting in seconds, then circulate your meeting notes to attendees right away at the end of the meeting.

Additional collaboration options

Drive communication and collaboration with Teams and SharePoint integrations.

Your Smart Fix: Ricoh IWB Add-On Service for Office 365

Elevate your meeting room setup with the cloud-based features of Ricoh IWB Add-On Service for Microsoft Office 365. This service offers a range of secure and integrated features that are designed to improve meeting collaboration and productivity.

Here are some key highlights:

  • Card authentication for secure access to Ricoh Interactive Whiteboard and Microsoft Office 365

  • Outlook Scheduler, Teams, and SharePoint integration for immediate meeting kick-off

  • Outlook Email integration to share meeting notes in one click

  • OneDrive for Business to retrieve meeting files and save as a native format

Why settle for just a basic setup when you can take it to the next level? It’s time to make every meeting count. Upgrade your meeting room experience with Ricoh IWB Add-on Service for Microsoft Office 365 today and improve collaboration, one meeting at a time.
