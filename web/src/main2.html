Support Your Hybrid Workforce

When work can be anywhere, it changes the way we work, communicate, and collaborate. How do you keep people connected and engaged? How do you retain the culture of your business? How do you ensure business continuity in times of rapid change?

Achieve your vision with our expertise

We design, integrate, and support your hybrid workforce with a holistic approach in creating a seamless digital workplace environment that will nurture employee engagement, facilitate a more dynamic way of working, and help your employees reach new performance levels. Our extensive solutions portfolio and ecosystem of communication and collaboration tools, and an integrated workspace management platform, will bring your digital workspace to greater heights. We have the expertise to help you build and scale your hybrid workplace.

Ricoh’s Hybrid Workplace know-how ensures that your team—physical or remote—has everything they need to work.

Creating the best of both worlds

To retain the best of both your physical and online workplace, you need the expertise, strategy, and technology to deploy tools that can connect people seamlessly, enable productive collaboration, and manage your physical workspace.

Let’s build your hybrid workplace

Not only does your modern hybrid workplace solutions need to deliver what your employees want—flexibility, agility, and mobility—but they also need to ensure business continuity through security, compliance, and standardised procedures. We understand the complexity in deploying hybrid working solutions to meet the requirements of your new digital reality. Our teams are dedicated to help you embark on a new journey, by developing a thorough understanding of your workforce, your current technology stack, and what is needed to achieve your hybrid workplace vision.

Ricoh and Microsoft Partnership
Microsoft Teams Rooms

In the hybrid workplace, how do we ensure all attendees—whether in offices or remote—can fully participate in meetings? Many room systems are designed primarily for people in the room, with remote users feeling left on the outside looking in. Microsoft Teams Rooms are designed to deliver inclusive meeting experiences with all participants in mind. With a rich ecosystem of connected meeting devices powered by an intuitive app built for Microsoft Teams, Teams Rooms turn any meeting spaces into an easy-to-use and inclusive collaboration space where everyone has a seat at the table, regardless of where they sit.

Ricoh has a strategic global relationship with Microsoft and extensive experience deploying Microsoft Teams Rooms solutions. We can help you modernise your workspaces, enhance meeting room experiences, and ensure your people stay focused on the business instead of being burdened by meeting room logistics.
