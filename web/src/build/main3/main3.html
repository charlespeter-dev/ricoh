Transform your meeting spaces with Microsoft Teams Rooms

Microsoft Teams Rooms are designed to deliver inclusive meeting experiences with all participants in mind. With a rich ecosystem of connected meeting devices powered by an intuitive app built for Microsoft Teams, Teams Rooms turn any meeting room into an easy-to-use and inclusive collaboration space where everyone has a seat at the table, regardless of where they sit.

Ease to use

A consistent user experience for all your spaces, from small huddle to large conference rooms – with a single touch, you get your meetings started.

Work like you are in one place

If you are on the go or attend a meeting in person, the rich collaboration experience keeps everyone engaged, and with advanced whiteboard technologies, ideas can just flow.

Transform your spaces

Enjoy a high-quality experience with the flexibility of choosing from a broad range of purpose-built, reliable options for your workplaces.

Simple to deploy on scale and easy to manage

Deploy, manage, and secure your rooms in a simple and scalable way with the Teams Admin Center or your existing Windows Management tools.

Overcome barriers to connecting with landlines and mobiles by enabling PSTN (Public Switched Telephone Network) calling from Microsoft Teams, which connects you to the Microsoft Phone System and our carrier network.

With Microsoft Teams Rooms operating as collaboration hubs, people can actively connect in real-life to work together on any device, from wherever they are. A phone system designed for the cloud offers:

  • All-in-one communication

    that unifies chat, files, meetings, apps, and calls

  • VoIP calls for every user

    so that all Teams users can make and receive VoIP calls for true collaboration

  • Seamless PSTN integration

    by adding dial tone (PSTN) access to users using Microsoft Teams Cloud Calling
