Our Intranet and Teams Solutions

SharePoint Online

SharePoint Online excels in offering a wide range of out-of-the-box functionalities. With Ricoh and SharePoint Online, you can provide your employees the means to easily customise an effective intranet for their respective departments, driving exceptional communication.

Features from SharePoint Online include:

  • A branded homepage
  • Multifunctional web parts that integrate with various Microsoft services
  • Custom navigation
  • Project and task scheduling
  • Flexible content sharing options
  • Modern/classic team and communication sites
  • Social features (communities, forums, media galleries)
  • Mobile access.

Microsoft Teams and Powell Teams Sync for Better Governance and Lifecycle Management

Microsoft Teams is a powerful collaboration tool but companies face some challenges in its adoption. These include uncontrolled creation of Teams channels, employees who don’t know how to fully use it, and ensuring everything is secure and compliant.

The best way forward is to effect better governance and lifecycle management, which is achievable by integrating Powell Teams with Microsoft Teams. This way, you can improve on teamwork in Microsoft Teams and obtain a collaboration experience that’s effortless yet easy to manage and govern.

For IT

IT Teams love Powell Teams, particularly for its governance framework. They are able to:

  • Secure best practices
  • Utilise the administration dashboard to control Teams creation and manage its lifecycle
  • Halve the number of Teams per employee and the number of harmful Teams, including those with guest and public access and inactive Teams.

For Employees

Employees can also enjoy an enhanced experience via:

  • A dashboard that helps them quickly find Teams
  • A virtual coffee machine
  • Ready-to-go templates
  • Simplified navigation.

Reduce the IT workload spent on Teams management by as much as 78% and make collaboration effortless and easier to manage and govern.

 

Benefits of Powell Teams and Microsoft Teams

Ready-to-set governance

With every IT rule in one place, your IT employees can choose and apply predefined strategies and policies to secure your Microsoft Teams environment and its best practices

Lifecycle management

Manage existing teams through an admin dashboard with a governance score, advanced reports, and guiding tips to take quick and efficient actions, helping you save up to 80% in time through automation

Controlled teams creation

Overcome Teams sprawl—let your users create the teams they need and approve it easily as a Powell Administrator
