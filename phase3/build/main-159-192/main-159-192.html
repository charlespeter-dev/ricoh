Without the right capabilities in place, effective business can be hindered by two factors – time-consuming manual data processes and the maintenance of rigorous compliance to standards and protocols.

Learn how Ricoh helps businesses make sense out of unstructured and structured data via Capture AI technology, cloud-based computing, and ML and supervised ML capabilities.

Focus on moving forward with an improved business-wide synergy and human resources that zero in on critical tasks. Intelligent Capture services include:

ABBYY FlexiCapture (AFC) Platform

ABBYY FlexiCapture (AFC) goes beyond as an intelligent data capture and extraction solution. Built for the needs of today’s complex digital enterprise, it brings together the best Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and advanced recognition abilities onto a single, enterprise-scale capture platform.

AFC intelligently captures, classifies, and transfers critical data from unstructured and structured documents to the right process, workflow, or decision engine. It can handle every type of document and job size at any stage, from acquisition to delivery.

With Ricoh and AFC, have better focus on your customer experience, cost reduction, compliance, and competitive advantage.
