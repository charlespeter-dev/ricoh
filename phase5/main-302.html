Your Vision, Empowered by Our Expertise

Our Regional Operations Centre Sections

Ricoh’s Regional Operations Centre consists of three key sections: Network Operations Centre, Service Delivery Operations, and Customer Excellence Centre.

Network Operations Centre

IT managers often grapple with the complexities of managing network infrastructure. The constant demand for smooth operation and optimal performance can easily become overwhelming. Critical components such as firewalls, routers, switches, access points, and servers require continuous attention to ensure seamless functionality.

The real challenge lies in identifying and resolving potential issues before they have a chance to disrupt the network. This is where a Network Operations Centre (NOC) equipped with real-time proactive monitoring steps in.

By leveraging advanced monitoring technologies, the NOC becomes the guardian of your network, constantly vigilant and on the lookout for any signs of trouble. It acts as a proactive defense system, detecting and addressing issues before they can cause significant disruptions. With round-the-clock monitoring, you can rest assured knowing that potential problems are being identified and resolved promptly.

Key offerings

  • Network & Infrastructure
  • Cloud Management
  • SASE Solutions

Service Delivery Operations

ROC’s Service Delivery Operations (SDO) is a centralised hub for delivering and enabling Managed IT Services. Operating 12x7 with the ability to provide 24x7 coverage when necessary, SDO efficiently handles a wide range of incidents and requests.

From individual IT technical issues to complex company-wide problems, SDO provides comprehensive support for the Ricoh APAC Digital Services Portfolio. By closely collaborating with OpCos’ local service desks, it ensures a seamless and exceptional customer experience. With a strong commitment to maintaining stable, cost-effective, and relevant IT infrastructure, SDO plays a crucial role in facilitating optimal performance and success for businesses throughout the Asia Pacific region.
